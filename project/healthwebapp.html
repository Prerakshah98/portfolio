A web portal that can monitor the prediction of health diseases can be beneficial for many people to save time by predicting the presence of a disease on their own. These automated services can help physicians in diagnosing diseases.

Created a website that helps patients diagnose the disease by entering their symptoms. I analyzed the datasets for the diseases and preprocessed the datasets that can be used in the model building. I implemented various machine learning algorithms and deep learning algorithms on the datasets.
